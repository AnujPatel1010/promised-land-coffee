Our Mission

A Light on a Hill

You are the light of the world. A city set on a hill cannot be hidden.

Matthew 5:14

Our long-term mission isn't just to create a coffee shop — it's to ship freshly roasted beans to churches nationwide on a weekly basis, with a portion of every order going directly to evangelism organizations across the world.

Until then, we're serving people right where they are — on social media — preparing for the day this community gathers in a coffee shop of its own. Everything we make points to two simple loves: the good news of Jesus and excellent coffee.
